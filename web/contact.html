Frequently asked questions
How is my footprint estimated?

We combine your activity inputs with category-specific emission factors to create a daily estimate.

Can I change an old calculation?

Create a new snapshot from the calculator. Your previous records remain available in History.

How do recommendations work?

Recommendations are prioritized by potential impact, effort, and the categories where you emit the most.
