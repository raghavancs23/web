About Project

Bridging Artificial Intelligence
& Sustainability

EcoTrack empowers individuals to measure their impact, adapt their behaviors, and actively combat climate change through actionable carbon offsets and intelligence.

Project Objective

To guide users to net-zero lifestyles using personalized data modeling and Gemini AI reasoning.

Why track with EcoTrack?

Most footprint models generalize. EcoTrack uses your hyper-local everyday choices in travel, home utilities, nutrition, and shopping habits to render a precise, interactive model of your dynamic carbon levels.

AI Recommendation Core

Google Gemini analyzes your lowest performing category and creates granular, easy-to-follow energy, transportation, and waste adaptation challenges.

Continuous Log History

Track variables daily, filter records to compare previous seasons, and export fully verified PDF emission statements for audits or resumes.

Global Gamified Leaderboard

Compete with friends, earn green carbon badges, and move up local town or city rankings as you reduce your weekly emissions.
